About Shaaz Hardware

Port Louis' Trusted
Hardware Depot

Located in the heart of Port Louis at 95 Royal Road, Shaaz Hardware has been the go-to destination for contractors, tradespeople, and passionate DIY enthusiasts across Mauritius. We are a full-service hardware depot stocking an extensive range of tools, equipment, and construction supplies to meet every project demand — big or small.

From concrete mixers and handheld grinders to nail shooters, LED panels, and complete DIY tool kits, our shelves are packed with everything you need to work smarter, build better, and finish faster. We take pride in offering genuine products at competitive prices, backed by friendly, expert advice from our experienced team.
